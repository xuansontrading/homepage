Our Story

The flavors we
never left behind

Xuân Sơn Commercial & Services Trading was born from pride — in Vietnamese heritage, in the flavors we grew up with, and in the belief that they deserve a place on shelves far beyond our own communities.

Founded by a new generation of Vietnamese professionals, our story is personal. For us, Vietnamese food is more than cuisine. It is memory. It is family gathered around a table, early mornings in Hà Nội, the bold soul of Sài Gòn's street corners, the quiet depth of Central Vietnam's ancient flavors — and the smell of a slow-cooked broth that no recipe could ever fully capture. As first- and second-generation Vietnamese Americans, we carry these moments with us — and we never want to lose them.

But life in the United States moves fast. The long hours, the daily rush, the distance between people who were once never far apart. What we miss most is not just the food — it is the time the food used to create. The unhurried mornings. The meals that turned into conversations. The table that always had room for one more.

That is what drives everything we do.

We work closely with trusted manufacturers and regional artisans across Vietnam to bring authentic, carefully curated products to American homes — from handcrafted fermented pastes to premium ready-to-eat meals, all held to the highest standards of quality, FDA compliance, and thoughtful presentation. Our ready-to-eat line is not about convenience for its own sake. It is about giving families their time back — so the table can be full again, even on a Tuesday night.

We are building more than a product line. We are telling a story — of Vietnam's regional diversity, its extraordinary culinary depth, and the people behind every flavor. We are reintroducing Vietnamese food not just as something familiar, but as something the world has been waiting to truly discover.
